Uploading!!!

You made it!

The final step of QMK firmware is quite simple. The first thing you need to do is plug in your keyboard while holding down the boot button.

This is usually a small black button on the microcontroller, but you may need to temporarily short two pads. Alternatively, if bootmagic is supported on your keyboard, holding down the key at (0,0) on the matrix will put the keyboard into bootloader mode.

However you do it, hold the button/connection for a few seconds after plugging in. In a moment, a folder will pop open.

With that, your rotary encoder is configured! Read the guide above for more information and uses.
