What is EasyQMK, you ask?

EasyQMK is an easy, simple, and straightforward complier for custom keyboards, macropads, or really anything with keys on a matrix! With my simple guide, you'll be setting up your keyboard, learning to compile, and finally how to upload the data to your keyboard.

What this guide assumes:

  1. That you're making this either from your imagination or from a design not a preset at this site (check the keyboard dropdown at the top)
  2. You know how matrices work - Don't?
  3. You know what your keyboard's "brain" is: the most common are the ATmega 32U4 and the RP2040.
